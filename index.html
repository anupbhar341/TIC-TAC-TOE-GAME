Tic Tac Toe

1st Player Turn

Rules:

  1. The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  2. Players take turns. Player 1 uses "X" and Player 2 uses "O". Click on an empty square to place your mark.
  3. The first player to align three of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the game.
  4. If all 9 squares are filled and no player has three marks in a row, the game ends in a tie.
  5. You can reset the game at any time by clicking the "Reset" button.
